# Chuba Makan Recipe App

**Chuba Makan** is a cooking application developed to provide users with diverse recipes from various cuisines worldwide, complete with estimated cooking times. The app not only offers a collection of recipes but also enables users to create and save their own recipes, catering to personalized culinary preferences.

## Features
- 📖 **Explore Recipes** – Discover a variety of dishes from different cultures.
- ⏳ **Cooking Time Estimates** – Plan meals with preparation and cooking durations.
- ✍️ **Create & Save Recipes** – Personalize your cooking journey by adding your own recipes.
- 🔍 **Search & Filter** – Quickly find recipes by ingredients, cuisine type, or cooking time.

## Built With
- **FlutterFlow** – A no-code/low-code platform for building Flutter applications.
- **Firebase** – Used for authentication, storage, and database management.

## Access the App
You can check out the app here: https://app.flutterflow.io/project/chuba-makan-wkjbon
